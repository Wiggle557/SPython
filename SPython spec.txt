SPython features:

- Pretty much all arithmetic operations 
- Variables (Strings or integers) 
- Print statements 
- While loops 
- Comparisons

Notable absences and rules:
- There is no and/ or with while statements
- Varibles cannot be empty nor can they be a string of length 0 
- Strings must be declared with 'text' not "text"
- There are no tuples or lists 
- Indentation must be 4 spaces
